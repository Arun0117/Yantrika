Watery Rocket

Note :"Watery Rocket event," teams build rockets from standard 2-liter plastic bottles, fill them with water, and launch them using pressurized air, aiming for the greatest time aloft or distance. Key rules include using only water and air pressure for propulsion, adhering to weight and size limits, and ensuring rockets remain intact during flight.
